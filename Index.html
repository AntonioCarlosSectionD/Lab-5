Genres

Indie

Indie music is a genre produced with an emphasize on creative freedom and authenticity. This is because they are produced independently from major record labels. This music, initially defined with the method of production, is associated with its distinctive non-mainstream aesthetic.

Electronic/dance

Electronic/dance music is a genre that is produced with electronic instuments and software. It is popular in clubs and festivals because it energizes listeners with its rhythm and beats. Another distinct quality of the music is its repetitive patterns and bass-heavy sounds.

Jazz

Jazz music is a genre well-known for its improvisation, swing rhythms, expressive melodies, and its deep roots in African American culture. It features intruments such as saxophones, trumpets, pianos, drums, and guitar. As stated, jazz is best known for its improvisation as each performance has musicians play off each other in real time.
